I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Milan in 2019 and my M.Sc. in Molecular Biotechnology and Bioinformatics from the University of Milan in 2015.

Currently, I work as Genomics Data Scientist at enGene Statistics, where my role is to disentangle, integrate, analyze and visualize complex multi-omics data and to develop Shiny web application to facilitate the process of investigation and hypothesis generation. To know more about me, you can have a look at my LinkedIn profile.

My motto is: "The answer is there, blowing in the bulk". If I am not on the shell, probably you find me on the Swiss alps.
